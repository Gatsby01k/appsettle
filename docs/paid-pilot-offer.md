# Settlement Proof Pilot — 30 days

A sellable, fixed-scope pilot offer. Derived from `controlled-live-test-readiness.md`,
`commercial-partner-terms.md`, and `pilot-readiness-index.md`. Sandbox-only;
INRSettle never moves funds.

## Who it's for

One operations or treasury lead at an INR payout provider, OTC desk, on/off-ramp,
or PSP that already runs payouts through third-party providers and needs to prove
settlement finality to partners, auditors, or internally.

## What you get

- A private workspace for your organization (role-based, dual-control).
- Your real settlements modeled through the proof chain: provider proof →
  independent reconciliation → audit trail → finality review.
- Independent-reconciliation setup against your bank/PSP records.
- A signed-off **settlement report** per settlement.
- A day-30 exceptions & finality summary, plus a 1-hour settlement-ops review readout.

## What INRSettle does

Records provider proof, requires and operates independent reconciliation, keeps an
immutable audit trail, runs a deterministic finality review under dual control, and
produces settlement reports and a provider/counterparty readiness assessment.

## What INRSettle does NOT do

Move, custody, route, price, or provide funds or liquidity. Execute payouts. Act as
an exchange, broker, P2P marketplace, or PSP. Perform end-user KYC/KYB. Guarantee
providers. Sandbox/test rails only throughout the pilot.

## Pricing

USD 2,000–5,000 fixed for the 30-day pilot (setup + review), creditable toward an
annual plan. (Anchor figure — adjust per account.)

## Onboarding steps

1. Scope call — corridor, providers, counterparty, caps, stop conditions.
2. Workspace + roles (operator + a separate approver for dual control).
3. Import a sample of real settlements and bank/PSP records.
4. Run the proof chain on 5–10 cases.
5. Day-30 readout.

## Success metrics

- % of settlements with a complete evidence chain.
- Exceptions / mismatches caught before finalization.
- Time-to-finality.
- Reports generated.
- Renewal intent ("would you keep paying").

## 30-day outcome

A reconciled, audited set of proven settlements, plus a written ops-review with the
gaps found and a finality-readiness verdict — the artifact you can show a partner or
auditor.
