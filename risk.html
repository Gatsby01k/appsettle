Risk Disclosure

Stablecoin-based settlement involves operational, regulatory, counterparty, liquidity, and technology risks.

Operational Risk

Settlement workflows may be affected by banking delays, network congestion, counterparty failure, reconciliation errors, or infrastructure incidents.

Regulatory Risk

Stablecoin, payment, treasury, and virtual asset rules differ by jurisdiction and may change over time.

Liquidity Risk

Execution quality and availability depend on market liquidity, operating limits, corridor conditions, and counterparty capacity.

No Investment Service

INRSettle is designed for business settlement operations and does not provide investment advice or consumer trading services. INRSettle is not presented as a retail exchange, investment product, or consumer crypto platform. Final legal and regulatory interpretation must be reviewed by qualified counsel for each operating jurisdiction.
