The problem

“Completed” is what the provider says. Not proof the money settled.

When a payout provider marks a payment completed, your settlement still isn’t final. You still need the provider’s proof, a matching bank or PSP record, an audit trail, and someone to approve finality.

Completed isn’t final

A provider marking a payout completed doesn’t prove the money actually arrived.

No independent check

Without a matching bank or PSP record, you’re trusting one side’s word.

No clear audit trail

When a payout is questioned later, you can’t show what happened, step by step.

Manual reconciliation

Spreadsheets and chat messages can’t prove a settlement is final.
